Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 July 2026

Graphe is a Bible-memorization app. This page explains what data the app handles and the choices you have. Questions? Email razondeltodo@gmail.com.

In short: you can use Graphe fully offline as a guest with no account and nothing about you leaves your device except the Bible text we fetch for the translation you pick. We do not sell your data, show ads, or use third-party advertising trackers.

What the app stores

On your device (always). Your memorization progress, plans, streaks, settings, and unlocked cosmetics are stored locally on the device.

In your account (only if you sign in). If you create an account, that same progress is backed up to our backend so you can restore it on another device, along with your username / display name and — if you use the social features — your friends, friend requests, group challenges, and activity feed. Authentication is handled by Supabase (email, and optionally Google or Apple sign-in).

AI features

When you use an AI feature (generating a study plan or Bible quiz, or a verse reflection), the topic or request you type is sent to our server, which relays it to our AI provider to generate the response. These prompts are used only to produce your result. Don't enter sensitive personal information into AI prompts. AI features can be turned off entirely in Settings → AI.

Analytics

For signed-in users we record lightweight, non-advertising usage events (for example, which screens are opened and when a lesson is completed) to understand how the app is used and to improve it. Guests are not recorded.

Permissions

  • Microphone & speech recognition — only for the "Speak the Verse" game, to score your recitation. Processed on-device; audio is not stored or uploaded.
  • Photos (optional) — only if you choose a profile picture from your library.
  • Notifications (optional) — for study reminders and the daily verse.

Third parties

  • Supabase — authentication and data storage for signed-in users.
  • AI provider — processes AI-feature prompts to generate plans, quizzes, and reflections.
  • Bible-text providers (such as OpenBible, Biblia, and YouVersion) — to fetch verse text for the translations you choose.

None of these receive advertising or tracking data from us.

Your choices & rights

  • Use without an account — stay a local guest; nothing leaves your device except verse-text fetches.
  • Sync — your account data syncs across your devices when signed in.
  • Delete everything — Settings → Account → Delete account permanently erases your account and all associated data from our backend and wipes it from the device. This is irreversible.

Children

Graphe is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children beyond what is described above.

Changes

We will update this page and the "Last updated" date when this policy changes.

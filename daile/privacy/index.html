Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 July 2026

Daile is a reading-diary app. This policy explains what it stores and shares, and the choices you have. Questions? Email razondeltodo@gmail.com.

In short: you can use Daile fully as a guest, and nothing you write leaves your device. Nothing is shared with other readers unless you choose to share it. We do not sell your data, show ads, or use third-party advertising trackers.

What the app stores

On your device (always). Your library of books and reading plans, daily reading logs, reflections (notes and quotes), streak, and app settings. In demo / guest mode this data never leaves your device.

In your account (only if you sign in). The same reading data, plus your display name, handle, bio, and optional profile photo, synced to our backend (Supabase) so it is available across your devices.

What is shared with other users

Nothing is shared unless you choose to share it:

  • A reflection is only visible to others if you turn on "Share to feed" when saving it.
  • If you mark a shared reflection as a spoiler, it is hidden behind a warning for readers who haven't reached that page.
  • Other users can see your public profile (name, handle, bio, photo), follow you, like your shared posts, and comment on them.

You can stop sharing at any time and delete your data with Reset all data in Settings.

Third-party services

  • Supabase — authentication, database, and photo storage (only when signed in).
  • Google Books API — book search (title, author, cover, description, ratings). Search queries are sent to Google; no account data is included.

None of these receive advertising or tracking data from us.

Device permissions

  • Notifications — only to deliver the daily reading reminder you enable.
  • Photo library — only when you choose a profile photo.
  • Microphone / dictation — handled entirely by your device's keyboard if you dictate a note; Daile does not record or store audio.

Data deletion

Use Reset all data in Settings to erase local data. To delete a cloud account and its data, see our account-deletion page or email us at the address below.

Children

Daile is suitable for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children beyond what is described above.

Changes

We will update this page and the "Last updated" date when this policy changes.

Contact

Questions about your data: razondeltodo@gmail.com.

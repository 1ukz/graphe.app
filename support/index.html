Frequently asked

How do I back up or move my progress to a new phone?

Create an account (Profile → sign in). Your plans, streaks, and progress sync to your account and restore automatically when you sign in on another device.

Can I use the app without an account?

Yes. Everything works offline as a guest; your data stays on your device.

How do I delete my account and data?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete account. This permanently erases your account and all associated data. It cannot be undone. See the account-deletion page for full details.

The "Speak the Verse" game won't hear me.

Make sure microphone and speech-recognition permission is granted in your device settings. You can also tap "I can't recite now" to type the verse instead.

How do I turn off the AI features?

Open Settings → AI and switch them off; all AI surfaces disappear from the app.

An AI plan or quiz won't generate.

This usually means a temporary network hiccup — try again in a moment. If it keeps failing, email us with your device model and language.

Features

A reading companion that adapts to you.

Not a fixed quota you'll fall behind on — a pace that recalculates every day.

Smart daily pacing

Set a finish date, your reading days, and your pace — Daile computes today's pages from what's left, adapting as you get ahead or fall behind.

Rest days, done right

Pick the weekdays you actually read. On a rest day you get a calm "Rest day 🌙" card — and your streak never breaks for resting.

"Life happens" replan

Fell behind? Replan logs what you managed and re-paces the remaining pages instantly — keeping your streak and your target date.

Instant book search

Add any book by title, author, or ISBN via Google Books — cover, page count, rating, and description, ready in a tap.

Reflections & feed

Save quotes and thoughts, and optionally share to a reading feed. Follow readers, like and comment — a real, gentle community.

Spoiler-safe sharing

Mark a shared reflection as a spoiler and it stays hidden behind a warning — auto-revealing only once a reader passes that page.

Paces remaining pages from today

You set the preferences. Daile does the math.

You never store a hard "pages per day". Daile recomputes the goal every time you open the app, always aimed at landing on your target date — and tells you whether you're ahead, on track, or behind.

  • A big reading day lowers tomorrow's goal; a skipped day nudges it up.
  • The daily reminder only fires on reading days — never on your rest days.
  • Everything works offline in guest mode — sign in only to sync and follow readers.
  • Dictate notes with your keyboard mic — Daile never records audio.
Private by design

Nothing is shared unless you share it.

Your library, logs, and unshared notes stay on your device in guest mode. A reflection reaches the feed only when you turn on “Share to feed”. No ads, no ad-trackers — book searches go to Google Books with no account data attached.

Delete your data & account

Last updated: 22 July 2026

You're always in control of your data in Daile. This page explains how to erase it — on your device and in the cloud — and exactly what gets removed.

Erase local data (guest or signed-in)

  1. Open Daile and go to Settings.
  2. Tap Reset all data.
  3. Confirm. Your books, plans, reading logs, reflections, streak, and settings are wiped from the device.

This is irreversible. Local data that was never synced to an account cannot be recovered once reset.

Delete your cloud account

If you signed in and want your account and all synced data permanently removed from our backend, email razondeltodo@gmail.com from the address associated with your account (or include your handle) and ask us to delete your account. We'll verify ownership and complete the deletion, typically within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and authentication record.
  • Your public profile — display name, handle, bio, and profile photo.
  • Your synced library, reading plans, logs, and reflections (including shared posts, likes, comments, and follows).
  • Any push-notification token linked to your account.

What is not stored after deletion

Daile does not use third-party advertising or cross-app tracking, so there is no advertising profile to remove. Dictation is handled by your device's keyboard — Daile never records or stores audio. Book searches sent to the Google Books API carry no account data.

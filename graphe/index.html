Features

A real memorization journey.

Built around how memory actually works — calm on the surface, seriously capable underneath.

Spaced repetition

An adaptive scheduler brings each verse back at the perfect moment to move it into long-term memory.

40+ memory games

Word Bank, Fill-in-the-Blank, First-Letter Recall, Scrambled Word, Typing Recall and many more, so practice never gets stale.

Speak the Verse

Recite a passage aloud and get scored instantly. Speech is transcribed on-device — your audio is never uploaded.

Any passage, any plan

Pick a verse, a chapter, or a whole book. Graphe builds a gentle personalized plan — plus a library of curated collections.

Friends & leaderboards

Add friends, share a leaderboard, and memorize a plan together in study groups — gentle motivation, never guilt.

Live group games

Kahoot-style group play: one screen hosts the questions, everyone else plays along in real time, round by round.

One minute a day. Verses that last a lifetime.

Graphe is quiet by design — no streak guilt, no noise, just a peaceful daily practice that fits the in-between moments of your day.

  • Contemplative lessons designed for the pauses in your day.
  • Many translations, with public-domain versions you can download for full offline use.
  • Beautiful themes, gentle haptics, optional sound effects, and ambient soundscapes.
  • Yours everywhere — an optional free account syncs across your phones and unlocks friends.
Private by design

Your practice stays yours.

You can use Graphe fully offline as a guest with no account — nothing about you leaves your device except the Bible text for the translation you pick. No ads, no third-party ad trackers, and we never sell your data. AI features are optional and can be turned off entirely.

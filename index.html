The approach

Built the way we'd want to be treated.

The same principles run through everything we make.

Private by default

Use either app fully as a guest — nothing leaves your device. No ads, no third-party ad trackers, no selling your data. Ever.

Local-first & offline

Your library, progress, and streaks live on your device and keep working with no signal. An optional free account only adds sync and friends.

Calm, not clingy

No streak guilt, no dark patterns, no noise. Just a peaceful daily practice designed to fit the in-between moments of your day.

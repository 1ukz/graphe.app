Frequently asked

How does Daile decide how many pages I read today?

You set a target finish date, the weekdays you read, and your pace. Daile paces the remaining pages across your reading days, recomputing every time you open the app — so a big day lowers tomorrow's goal and a skipped day nudges it up, always aiming at your target date.

Can I use the app without an account?

Yes. In guest mode everything works offline and your data stays on your device. Sign in only if you want to sync across devices and use the reading feed.

Will resting break my streak?

No. Pick your reading weekdays and rest days are first-class: you'll see a calm "Rest day 🌙" card, the daily reminder won't fire, and your streak is safe. Reading ahead on a rest day counts as a bonus.

I fell behind — what do I do?

Use Replan. It logs what you managed and instantly re-paces the remaining pages, keeping your streak and your target date.

How do I back up or move my library to a new phone?

Sign in (Profile → sign in). Your books, plans, logs, and reflections sync to your account and restore when you sign in on another device.

My daily reminder isn't arriving.

Make sure notifications are enabled for Daile in your device settings, and that a reminder time is set in Settings. Remember the reminder only fires on your chosen reading days — not on rest days.

A book won't show up in search.

Search uses the Google Books API. Try the title plus author, or the ISBN. If it still doesn't appear, that title may not be in Google's catalogue — email us and we'll help.

How do I delete my data or account?

Use Reset all data in Settings to erase local data, or see the account-deletion page to remove your cloud account and everything in it.

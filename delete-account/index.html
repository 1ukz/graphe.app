Delete your account & data

Last updated: 21 July 2026

You can permanently delete your Graphe account and everything associated with it at any time. This page explains how, and exactly what gets removed.

Delete from within the app (recommended)

  1. Open Graphe and go to Settings → Account.
  2. Tap Delete account.
  3. Confirm. Your account and all associated data are permanently erased from our backend and wiped from the device.

This is irreversible. Once deleted, your progress, plans, streaks, friends, and profile cannot be recovered.

Prefer to request it by email?

If you can't access the app, email razondeltodo@gmail.com from the address associated with your account (or include your username / display name) and ask us to delete your account. We'll verify ownership and complete the deletion, typically within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and authentication record.
  • Your username / display name and profile picture.
  • Your memorization progress, plans, streaks, and settings held in the account.
  • Your social data — friends, friend requests, group challenges, and activity feed entries.
  • Any push-notification token linked to your account.

What is not stored after deletion

Graphe does not use third-party advertising or cross-app tracking, so there is no advertising profile to remove. "Speak the Verse" audio is processed on-device and is never uploaded, so nothing about it exists on our servers.

Guest data

If you never created an account, your data only ever lived on your device. Deleting the app (or using Settings → Account to clear local data) removes it completely.
